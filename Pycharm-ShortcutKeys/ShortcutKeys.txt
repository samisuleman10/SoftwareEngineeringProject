PyCharm ide - Shortcut Keys

1-Double Shift	
  -Search Everywhere
  -Find anything related to PyCharm or your project and open it, execute it, or jump to it.

2-F2/Shift+F2
  -Navigate between code issues
  -Jump to the next or previous highlighted error.

3-Ctrl+/Ctrl+Shift+/	
  -Add/remove line or block comment
  -Comment out a line or block of code.
